## 4.4 Cryptographic significance
The cryptographic implications of Shor-type algorithms are a major reason why quantum algorithms have had impact beyond theoretical computer science. In particular, these results motivated sustained interest in post-quantum cryptography and in the long-term security consequences of scalable fault-tolerant quantum computing.

## 4.5 Practical barriers
Although the theoretical importance of these algorithms is unquestioned, their practical realization requires substantial quantum resources. As a result, their transformative implications remain tied to future progress in fault-tolerant hardware and error correction.
