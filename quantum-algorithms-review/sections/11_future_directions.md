## 11.1 Overview
The future of quantum algorithm research will depend not only on discovering new theoretical speedups but also on clarifying which algorithmic ideas can survive realistic resource constraints and deliver meaningful practical value.

## 11.2 Resource-aware algorithm design
One important direction is the design of algorithms that explicitly account for hardware limitations, noise, circuit depth, and fault-tolerant overhead. This shift may prove essential for narrowing the gap between abstract complexity results and implementable computation.
