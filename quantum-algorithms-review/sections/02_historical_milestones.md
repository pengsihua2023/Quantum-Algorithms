## 2.3 Breakthrough era: Shor and Grover
Shor’s factoring algorithm and Grover’s search algorithm are widely regarded as the two landmark breakthroughs that brought quantum algorithms to the center of computational research. Shor’s work showed that quantum computation could provide dramatic theoretical speedups for problems of major cryptographic relevance, while Grover’s algorithm established a more general quadratic improvement for unstructured search.
