# 6. Quantum Linear Algebra Algorithms

## 6.1 Overview
Linear algebraic tasks arise across scientific computing, optimization, and machine learning, making them a natural target for quantum algorithm design. This has led to the development of algorithms intended to accelerate linear system solving, matrix operations, and related numerical tasks.

Quantum algorithms for linear systems attracted major attention because they suggested the possibility of substantial speedups under certain assumptions.
