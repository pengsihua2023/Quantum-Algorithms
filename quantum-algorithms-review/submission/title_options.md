# Title Options

## Purpose
This file stores candidate titles for the review article and notes on their scope, tone, and suitability.

---

## Main Title Candidates

### Option 1
**Research Progress in Quantum Algorithms: Foundations, Major Advances, Practical Constraints, and Future Directions**

**Strengths**
- broad and balanced
- clearly signals both theory and practice
- suitable for a full review article

**Possible weakness**
- slightly long

---

### Option 2
**Quantum Algorithms: Foundations, Advances, and Challenges**

**Strengths**
- concise
- academic
- easy to remember
- works well for a broad review

**Possible weakness**
- somewhat generic

---

### Option 3
**A Review of Recent Progress in Quantum Algorithms**

**Strengths**
- classic review-paper title
- simple and direct
- clearly signals article type

**Possible weakness**
- less specific about structure and angle

---

### Option 4
**Quantum Algorithms in Review: Major Developments, Practical Barriers, and Open Problems**

**Strengths**
- emphasizes analytical balance
- highlights both progress and limitations
- suitable for a critical review

**Possible weakness**
- slightly more editorial in tone

---

### Option 5
**From Theoretical Speedups to Practical Constraints: Research Progress in Quantum Algorithms**

**Strengths**
- clearly foregrounds the theory-practice tension
- strong fit for this manuscript’s main argument
- distinctive and conceptually sharp

**Possible weakness**
- less neutral than a standard survey title

---

### Option 6
**Quantum Algorithms: A Structured Review of Foundational Results, Modern Developments, and Open Challenges**

**Strengths**
- signals organization and scope clearly
- suitable for a synthesis-heavy review
- strong academic tone

**Possible weakness**
- relatively long

## Shortlist Recommendations

**Quantum Algorithms: Foundations, Advances, and Challenges**

### Best classic review-paper choice
**A Review of Recent Progress in Quantum Algorithms**

### Best choice if you want the theory-practice tension to stand out
**From Theoretical Speedups to Practical Constraints: Research Progress in Quantum Algorithms**

### Best choice if you want a more structured and comprehensive tone
**Research Progress in Quantum Algorithms: Foundations, Major Advances, Practical Constraints, and Future Directions**

---

## Selection Notes
When choosing the final title, consider:

- Is the article mainly broad and descriptive, or also analytical and critical?
- Do you want a classic review-paper title or a more distinctive title?
- Do you want to foreground practical constraints in the title?
- Is the target journal likely to prefer short conservative titles?
