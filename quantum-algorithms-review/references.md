## 5. Notes on Citation Quality
When filling references:
- Prefer original foundational papers for first-discovery claims
- Prefer reputable reviews for broad summaries
- Prefer recent papers for current status statements
- Avoid unsupported claims about practical quantum advantage
- Do not cite papers that do not actually support the sentence

---

## 6. Final Citation Checks
Before submission:
- Verify every strong claim has support
- Check historical claims carefully
- Check complexity claims carefully
- Check that practical-performance statements are not overgeneralized
- Ensure no placeholder remains in the final version
